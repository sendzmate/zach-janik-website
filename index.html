Philosophy & Vision

On Business

"Everyone who is a successful business owner has the exact same brain as you and went to the same 12 years of school you did, they just had the balls to make the jump."

Business owners don't need more jargon. They need clarity, direction, and sometimes someone to sit them down and say, "This isn't working. Let's fix it."

On Motorcycles

"What matters is the ride. Not the brand. Not the price tag. Not the displacement. Just the ride."

The motorcycle world is dominated by advertising that creates subcultures and gatekeeps new riders. I want to remove the barriers to entry and increase access to good knowledge.
